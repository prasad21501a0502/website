RAJIV
PARK

Rajiv Park is a name that can refer to different parks in India. One of them is Rajiv Gandhi Park in Vijayawada, which is a beautiful park with exotic animals, dinosaurs replicas and a musical fountain1. Another one is Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park in Pune, which is a large zoo with various mammals, reptiles and birds23. It also has a wildlife research centre and a snake park. A third one is Rajiv Gandhi National Park in Rameswaram, which is a small national park with dry deciduous forest and wildlife4. All these parks are named after Rajiv Gandhi, the former Prime Minister of India. They are popular destinations for tourists and locals alike.
