PRAKASAM
BARRAGE

The Prakasam Barrage is a 1,223.5 meter long barrage that stretches across the Krishna River, connecting Vijayawada, NTR and Mangalagiri Tadepalle Municipal Corporation, Guntur districts in Andhra Pradesh, India 1. It was constructed during 1954 to 1957 in place of an old anicut just 104 feet upstream side near Vijayawada City in Krishna District of Andhra Pradesh 2. The barrage serves as a road bridge and spans over a lake 1. The three canals associated with the barrage run through the city of Vijayawada, crossing it and giving it a Venetian appearance
