BHAVANI
ISLAND

Bhavani Island is situated in the midst of the Krishna River, at Vijayawada. It is located at the upstream of Prakasam Barrage and is considered as one of the largest river islands in India, with an area of 133 acres1. This Island on the River Krishna is a marvellous picnic spot attracting visitors from all over the country. With umpteen facilities, Bhavani Island has emerged as one of the major attractions of Vijayawada2. The island has a robotic dinosaur park, garden maze, mirror maze, golf and badminton simulator, cycling, etc3. If you are planning to visit Bhavani Island, you can reach there by road or by boat. The nearest railway station to Bhavani Island is located in Vijayawada named Vijayawada Railway Station, which is about 7 kms from Bhavani-dweep
